Our Story

From a small coffee cart to Dar es Salaam's most beloved café. This is how it all began.

Since 2018

Born from a Love of Great Coffee

Ahlan Café & Restaurant started with a simple belief: everyone deserves an exceptional cup of coffee. What began as a modest coffee cart on Oyster Bay has grown into a full-service café and kitchen that the community calls home.

We source our beans directly from smallholder farmers in Mbeya and Kilimanjaro regions, paying fair prices that support local families. Our in-house roasting ensures every batch is fresh, and our kitchen team crafts each dish with the same care we put into every espresso.

Today, we're proud to be more than just a café. We're a workspace for freelancers, a meeting spot for friends, a quiet corner for readers, and a place where strangers become regulars. This is your Ahlan.
