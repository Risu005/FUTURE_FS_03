FAQ

Common Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We recommend booking for groups of 4 or more, especially on weekends. Use our contact form or WhatsApp to reserve your table.

Is there parking available?

We have dedicated parking for 12 cars right outside the café. Street parking is also available along Brew Street.

Do you offer catering?

Absolutely. We cater events of all sizes — from office meetings to weddings. Contact us for a custom quote.

Are pets allowed?

Well-behaved pets are welcome on our outdoor patio. Indoor seating is reserved for service animals only.
